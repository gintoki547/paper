# 摘要与关键词

## 摘要

《东京梦华录》较为细致地记录了北宋都城汴京的商业贸易、饮食消费、服饰制度、娱乐活动及岁时民俗，是研究宋代城市生活的重要文献。但由于原文以笔记体叙述为主，信息分布较散，普通读者在阅读时较难快速建立清晰的时间、空间和社会生活图景。本课题以《东京梦华录》为主要研究对象，结合相关文献资料，对其中具有视觉转译价值的内容进行提取、分类和整理，并从时间、空间、人群、物象和行为等维度建立信息结构。在设计实践中，以“汴京烟火”为主题，将历史文本、数据图表和场景插画相结合，完成包括汴河晨食、相国万货、锦绣衣冠、瓦舍百戏、州桥夜食和岁时节序在内的六张系列信息图。通过本次设计，尝试以更直观的视觉方式呈现宋代市民生活与城市文化，为古籍内容的信息可视化表达提供一定参考。

## 关键词

《东京梦华录》；信息可视化；视觉传达设计；宋代文化

## Abstract

*The Eastern Capital: A Dream of Splendor* records various aspects of urban life in Bianjing, the capital of the Northern Song Dynasty, including commerce, food consumption, clothing systems, entertainment activities and seasonal customs. However, as the original text is mainly written in the form of notes, its information is scattered and difficult for general readers to understand in a direct and systematic way. Taking *The Eastern Capital: A Dream of Splendor* as the main research object, this project extracts, classifies and organizes the visualizable information from the text and related literature. Based on the dimensions of time, space, social groups, objects and behaviors, an information structure is established for visual translation. With the theme of “Bianjing Life and Urban Vitality”, the design combines historical text, data charts and scene illustrations, and finally forms a series of six infographics, including morning food along the Bian River, markets at Xiangguo Temple, clothing and social hierarchy, entertainment in Washe, night food near Zhouqiao, and seasonal festivals. Through this design practice, the project attempts to present the daily life and urban culture of the Song Dynasty in a clearer visual form, and provides a reference for the information visualization of ancient texts.
